Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT)

Mega Sessions up to 4,000 grafts.

Also known as The Strip hair transplant procedure, FUT is hair restoration surgery that enables MAXiM hair transplant surgeons to perform mega sessions up to 4,000 grafts.

DurationProcedure takes 5 to 7 hours.
